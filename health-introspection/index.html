Introspection the activity and the motivation

2018-10-22 | 2019-11-12 | murmur
| 3.9k

Fall again and again, why every time I said that I want to achieve a new life, and all the sudden, the activity from ordered structure to the turbulence structure? I cannot help but thinking that I should do something wrong on strategy. Although I know this is not the last time that I write this kind of article, and I know this is like writing something useless. However, one day in the future, I might have a chance to see what I think in age 27.

¶Why I failed again?

Life won’t start to fail at the beginning, there should be some precursor and the nonlinear interaction between each decision that I made. As all the failing points come together at the same time, the rogue wave event occurs.Thus, I recorded several possible reasons that why I failed again for the management and do not focus on the present activity.

  • Spend lots of time on animation and Internet novel

    This is the result, not the reason that I spend a lot of time on this. The main reason is that I want to escape from the present life, I do not want to face the truth that I cannot handle all events as the perfect every time. It is pretty hard to face the truth, but you are really not that good. Do not think that you are wonderful. The only method to find the way is just do it.

  • Not self-confidence enough about myself

    As the previous understanding, I do not pay attention to myself confidence. I always think that I am not a good person, alway imply that should be better than this. I do not clip hands for myself, even for a small single thing like shows on time. But how to be a confidence person? This is a big question. Not everyone is the same and no one is the same. I think I have to be self-promoted and also encourage myself when I finished something. If I failed, do not be afraid, because I will always face struggle in the future. Failure is not an opinion, but failure is the only way to find success. Be careful, everyone fails, but not every successfully person show you how to face the fail.

  • Nerves life, easy to fail

    I should give myself a space to take a rest. That does not mean I can be addicted to the habit that I know I always stick on it (e.g. comics, novels). For the entertainment activities: singing songs, watching Youtube videos, doing house work, exercising .etc. There are many helpful things that I can do!

¶Make a wish for the present

Why I say present not the future? That is because I think people should live in the present, not the future. So I have to reach the goal 「right now」. How could that be? Just faith to success. There is no reason that I cannot reach the goal.

  • Write the weekly review and time recording

    Recently, I tried to throw this activity out of my life. Obviously, I just failed again. I cannot control myself doing the thing on the right track. So the only method is trying to let myself understand what should I do in present. This could be really tough and need to spend some time to accept this kind of life style, but I know I can take this kind of challenge. I have nothing to lose for testing this method. That is fine if I do not do the thing at the right time, but I still have to pay attention to the

  • Mental and physical health

    As before, do the meditation five days a week should be great for maintaining the life. Also, do the exercise for about 3 times a week. That should be the maximum I only can do for a week. How to get the target? Just follow the previous method.
