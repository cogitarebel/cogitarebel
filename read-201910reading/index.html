¶Mind

¶Hundred Little Decisions: Training Ourselves at the Decision Point

Start with a short pause at the beginning for each tasks. No matter it is watching youtube video, doing works, or even exercising. At the same time, ask yourself about if this activity is really helpful or just waste your time. There is no perfect in life, but you can try to do the best after practicing.

¶A Magical Way to Work with Our Habitual Patterns

I have to grateful for the past guardian, which accompanied with me in the past during the sadness, fear, and tough time period. But the procrastination habits are no longer needed anymore because i’m growing up. So I have to thank those habits and be grateful for their help in the best. Instead of regarding them as a bad man, regarding them as a nice experience should be easily for us to escape from the black hole.

¶Making peace with imperfection

The story of showing that being perfect is should be stressful and harmful for health and performance. Embrace the imperfaction and believe that you can still be a tough guy and improve yourself daily. As long as you understand the goal and the proper method to do that.

¶Discipline Challenge: What My Mind Does When I Commit to Hard Things

This is the feedback summary of the personal challenge from the blogger. He shows that he still can fall to do the staffs and habits. However, some of the habits can stick to the daily life. Life is complex, so I think I have to adjust the strategy in a short term. As long as you change the method to finish the stuff, it could lead to the better life quality.

¶Creating the Habit of Not Being Busy

  1. Prioritize high-impact tasks
  2. Connect to meaning.
  3. Focus on one small task at a time
  4. Let go of the narrative so you can focus.
  5. Focus with full presence, gratitude & meaning.
