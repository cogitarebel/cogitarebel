Grammar checking list

2019-12-24 | 雜記
| 4.2k

This checking list is mainly focused on the grammar checking for myself after I finished the articles. For the people who doesn’t use the English as their mother language, it is hard for us to write the article always with the correct grammar, which could be annoying. Therefore, I tried to develop a method to prevent this problem, check the article after finished with the checking list. Indeed, we are still blinded for our articles in grammar because of the focusing on the content.

¶Online Tools

  • Grammarly
    One of the best free grammar online checking tool. I always use this tool after I finished writing the email or formal articles. The major bugs such as the verbs, tense can be automatically checked. However, the advanced function such as the correctness and clearness of the article, you have to pay it for those services.
